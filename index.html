Characters
Guts, renowned as the "Black Swordsman", is a former mercenary and branded wanderer who travels the world in a constant internal struggle between pursuing his own ends.
Casca is a former unit commander of the Band of the Falcon and an estranged companion of Guts.
Griffith, renowned as the "White Falcon", is the leader of the reborn Band of the Falcon and Supreme Commander of the Midland Regular Army.
Puck is an elf of the Pisky race, descendants of the ancient spirits of wind. A former denizen of Elfhelm, he left out of sheer boredom and became Guts' first newfound companion since.
